Overview

My primary research interests are in Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence with an emphasis on Computer Vision. The main applications for my research activities focus on making meaningful inferences from sparse latent representations and optimizing feature selectors for use in representative learning. I am interested in investigating the applications of computer vision with expert systems to design complex tools for automation.
