MY RESEARCH

My primary research interests are in LiDAR pointcloud classification and part segmentation using Deep Learning.

My focal area is in the field of forest ecology, where I am interested in the use of LiDAR pointclouds to map forest biomass, carbon stocks, and biodiversity to help model and predict the effects of wildfires and climate change on forest ecosystems.
